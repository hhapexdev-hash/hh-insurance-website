Home/About

“We started at zero.”

Three people and no policies in 2018. Forty-five-plus licensed professionals today. The long version is below; the short version is: we answer the phone.

  • 1979

    The Holehouse family enters Florida insurance. The original family agency grows for decades before being acquired.

  • 2018

    Jake and his father Ron restart from scratch in St. Petersburg. Three people. Zero policies. All conviction.

  • 2022

    Business Observer names Jake to its 40 under 40. HH becomes Tampa Bay TV's regular explainer for storm and market coverage.

  • Today

    45+ teammates, service and sales awards, thousands of Florida households and businesses — and an in-house AI service platform we built ourselves.

What we believe

Three house rules

01

Tell the truth about coverage

If you're already well-insured, we say so. If a cheaper policy is worse, we show you exactly where. No scare tactics, ever.

02

Answer the phone

Phones open at 7am. Real humans, not a queue. Our reviews name the people who picked up — that's the whole point.

03

Stay nerdy

CPCU-led (a credential held by roughly the top 0.5% of the industry), a master's in risk management, and guides that make flood insurance make sense.
