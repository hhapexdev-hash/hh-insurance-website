05 · Voice

Plain English, named humans, receipts

Do

Specific beats super­lative

"40+ carriers, one quote" — never "best" or "cheapest." If we can't prove it, we don't say it.

Do

Name the humans

Reviews, bylines, specialists — people have names here. "Talk to a human" is the recurring CTA for a reason.

Don't

No scare tactics

Storm content informs, never frightens. We say "carriers pause binding" and explain why — not "act now before it's too late."
